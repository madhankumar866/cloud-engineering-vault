# 📚 **MLOps Roadmap with Resources (6 Months)**

---

## 📍 Month 1–2 → ML Foundations

**Week 1–2 (Python, Pandas, ML basics)**

- 📘 Book: _Python Data Science Handbook_ (Jake VanderPlas) → [Free PDF online]
- 🎥 Course: FreeCodeCamp “Python for Data Science” (YouTube, 4 hrs)
- 🔗 GitHub: Titanic Dataset ML notebooks (Kaggle Titanic Competition)

**Week 3–4 (scikit-learn & PyTorch basics)**

- 🎥 Course: _Intro to Machine Learning with scikit-learn_ (DataCamp free)
- 🎥 Course: _Deep Learning with PyTorch: Zero to GANs_ (FreeCodeCamp x Jovian)
- 🔗 Repo: [PyTorch MNIST Example](https://github.com/pytorch/examples/tree/main/mnist)

**Week 5–6 (Hugging Face Transformers)**

- 📘 Book: _Natural Language Processing with Transformers_ (O’Reilly, free on HF site)
- 🎥 YouTube: “Hugging Face Course” by Hugging Face (official channel)
- 🔗 Repo: [Hugging Face Course GitHub](https://github.com/huggingface/course)

**Week 7–8 (Project: FastAPI + Docker)**

- 🎥 YouTube: _FastAPI Crash Course_ (freeCodeCamp, 3 hrs)
- 📘 Guide: Dockerizing FastAPI apps

## 📍 Month 3 → ML Lifecycle & Versioning

**Week 9–10 (MLflow & DVC)**

- 📘 Docs: MLflow Quickstart
- 🎥 YouTube: “MLOps with MLflow” (Krish Naik channel)
- 📘 Docs: DVC Get Started
- 🔗 Repo: [DVC Examples](https://github.com/iterative/example-get-started)

**Week 11–12 (Project: Experiments + Versioning)**

- 🎥 Course: _Hands-on MLOps with MLflow & DVC_ (YouTube playlist by DataTalksClub)
- 🔗 Repo: [End-to-End MLflow Project](https://github.com/mlflow/mlflow-example)

## 📍 Month 4 → Deployment & Serving

**Week 13–14 (FastAPI + TorchServe)**

**Week 15–16 (Docker + K8s)**

- 📘 Book: _Kubernetes in Action_ (for deep dive, optional)
- 🎥 YouTube: “Kubernetes Crash Course for Beginners” (TechWorld with Nana, 1hr)
- 🔗 Repo: [Deploy ML on K8s](https://github.com/kubeflow/examples)
