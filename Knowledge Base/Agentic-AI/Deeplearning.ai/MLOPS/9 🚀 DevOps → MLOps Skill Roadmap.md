## 3. **Core MLOps Skills (your DevOps muscle + ML tools)**

Here’s where overlap happens:

- **Experiment Tracking & Reproducibility**
    - MLflow, DVC (like Git but for ML datasets/models)
- **Model Packaging & Deployment**
    - Dockerize models (Flask/FastAPI)
    - Deploy on Kubernetes (inference services)
    - Use KFServing, BentoML, or Seldon
- **Model CI/CD**
    - Automate retraining pipelines
    - Integrate ML workflows in Jenkins/GitHub Actions
- **Data & Model Monitoring**
    - Monitor model drift & data quality (EvidentlyAI, WhyLogs)
    - Use Prometheus + Grafana for inference latency, error rates

👉 This is the **sweet spot** where your DevOps background is a superpower.
